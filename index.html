About Me
Hi there! Welcome to my page... I'm Rachel. I grew up in a teeny, tiny town in the mountains of Arizona. I studied biology in college, but got my first grown-up job in marketing, so stuck with that for a while. I have been lucky enough to have lived and worked in England and Hawaii, but am now located in Philadelphia (go birds!). In my free time, I like to get outside and explore. I love scuba diving, hiking and checking out breweries in the city. I'm currently enrolled in the University of Arizona's coding bootcamp and am developing skills in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, Express.js, React.js, Node.js, Database Theory, MongoDB, MySQL, Command Line, Java, Git and more. I'm looking forward to learning these new skills and seeing where this next path in life takes me. Stay tuned...
